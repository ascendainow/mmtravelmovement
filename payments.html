Payment Policy

  • A non-refundable deposit is required to secure all bookings.
  • Full payment is typically due 60-90 days prior to departure.
  • Payment plans are available for most group and individual trips.
  • All prices are quoted in USD unless otherwise stated.
  • Pricing is subject to availability and may change until deposit is received.

Cancellation Policy

  • Deposits are non-refundable but may be transferable in some cases.
  • Cancellations 90+ days before departure: partial refund may apply.
  • Cancellations 60-89 days before departure: 50% of total trip cost.
  • Cancellations within 60 days: no refund (travel protection recommended).
  • Travel protection is strongly recommended for all bookings.

Travel Protection

  • Travel insurance is available through our preferred providers.
  • Coverage includes trip cancellation, medical, and baggage.
  • Protection plans start at approximately $50 per person.
  • We strongly recommend insurance for all international travel.
  • Contact Melody for plan options and pricing.
